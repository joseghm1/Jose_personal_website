About me!

I’m Jose Hernandez — a Software Engineer and Founder based in Florida, and a Computer Science graduate from UCF with a focus on AI and full-stack development. My passions lie in Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning, along with strong interests in physics, technology, e-commerce, and finance.

I enjoy building modern web applications, intelligent systems, and immersive 3D experiences that combine engineering and design. I also run AVAZ Import, an export business I founded in 2020, which I’m now transitioning into a modern e-commerce platform that streamlines access to U.S. products for customers around the world. I’m driven by a long-term mission to contribute to the AI revolution and create technology that pushes society forward.
